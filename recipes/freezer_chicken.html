Easy Freezer Chicken

Description:

This is an easy recipe for those who want an easy dinner. Simply add all the ingredients to a ziplock bag and freeze until needed. Then toss in crock pot for several hours and you have a delicious and easy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. Chicken, breasts or thighs
  • 1 lbs. peas
  • 2 tablespoons butter.
  • Lemon juice, black pepper, and garlic to taste.

How to make:

  1. Add all ingredients to gallon ziplock bag.
  2. Mix bag thoroughly.
  3. Put in freezer if preparing in advance, otherwise add bag to crockpot.
  4. Cook in crockpot for 8 hours on low setting.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
