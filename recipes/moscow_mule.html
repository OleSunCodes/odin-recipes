Refreshing Moscow Mule

Description

Refreshing moscow mule. Perfect for a warm summer day after the fun in the sun.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 oz vodka
  • Fresh lime and/or lime juice
  • 4-8 oz ginger beer
  • Copper mug
  • Ice

How to make

  1. Fill mug with ice.
  2. Add vodka to copper mug.
  3. Squeeze fresh lime into mug.
  4. Fill mug with ginger beer.
  5. Top with Sliced lime.
